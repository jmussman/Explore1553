# Lab 1: Introduction

## Research the following questions:

\[ [Lab Table of Contents](./README.md#labs) \]

As a group, or individually, research the following questions:

* Where was MIL-STD-1553 developed and why?
* What obstacles stood in the way of MIL-STD-1553? And who?
* What are the types of devices connected to MIL-STD-1553?
* What are "notices" to MIL-STD-1553? Why the name "notice"?
* Why does Notice 6 disallow direct connections?
* Why is 1Mbps MIL-STD-1553 still preferred over faster Ethernet?
* How do military airframes isolate failures to "limp along"?
* What airframe forced application of MIL-STD-1553?
