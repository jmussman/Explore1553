## Lab Steps

The scenario is that a Flight Computer or such a device often has the bus controller integrated into it.
Part of this includes the functionality to run a schedule and poll devices such as an altimeter, so the display
is constantly updated with the altitude and rate of change, etc.

This lab is not going to do anything quite so complicated, but it will provide a taste of how this is accomplished.
